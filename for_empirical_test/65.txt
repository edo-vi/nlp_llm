Mitratapide is a veterinary medication used for the treatment of overweight and obese dogs sold under the brand name Yarvitan Its mechanism of action involves enzyme inhibition
The drug was developed by Janssen Pharmaceutica and is chemically related to the Antifungal medication
Mitratapide (under the brand name Yarvitan) was authorized for use in the EU by the European Medicines Agency for helping weight loss in dogs but it has since been withdrawn from the market in the EU
