Cimicoxib (UR8880 trade name Cimalgex) is a nonsteroidal antiinflammatory drug (NSAID) used in veterinary medicine to treat dogs for pain and inflammation associated with osteoarthritis and for the management of pain and inflammation associated with surgery It acts as a COX2 inhibitor
