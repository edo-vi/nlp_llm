

Taxobox


Brucella ovis is a Gramnegative coccobacillus from the Brucellaceae family  Along with Brucella melitensis it is responsible for causing ovine brucellosis which is a notifiable disease B ovis can be transmitted by the stable fly Infection causes severe inflammation of the epididymis particularly the tail
