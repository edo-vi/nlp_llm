
Theriogenology is a specialty of veterinary medicine concerned with animal reproduction This includes the physiology and pathology of male and female reproductive systems of animals and the clinical practice of veterinary obstetrics gynecology andrology and assisted reproductive technologies (ART) Theriogenologists are veterinarians with advanced training in animal reproduction like semen analysis evaluation and processing Animal breeding
