
The UK Video Charts  is a group of charts compiled by the Official Charts Company on behalf of The British Association for Screen Entertainment The charts are based on weekly DVD Bluray and formerly VHS sales in 6500 retailers across the UK The main chart combines the sales of all video sales in the UK and trades under the name of the Combined Video Chart There are also charts for the sales of music videos (Music Video Chart) and childrens videos among others All of the charts are published on the OCCs website
