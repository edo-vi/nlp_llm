
The State Veterinary Service (SVS) was from 1938 to 2007 an executive agency of the Department for Environment Food and Rural Affairs (Defra) a Departments of the Government of the United Kingdom
The State Veterinary Department was set up in 1865 in response to an outbreak of rinderpest in London The State Veterinary Service was established in 1938 and was overseen by the Chief Veterinary Officer (United Kingdom)
