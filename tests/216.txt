Clamp in Wonderland is a series of music videos produced by Clamp (manga artists)
The short films feature characters from the groups entire canon of work The first spans Clamps career from their beginnings until the year 1994 The sequel Clamp in Wonderland 2 includes over 100 characters from the Clamp universe

The videos were released to DVD on October 26 2007 as Clamp in Wonderland 1&2 19892006 (BCBA3143)

Clamp in Wonderland features characters created between 1989 and 1994 and Clamp in Wonderland 2 features characters created between 1995 and 2006
