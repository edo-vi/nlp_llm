Firocoxib sold under the brand names Equioxx and Previcox among others is a nonsteroidal antiinflammatory drug of the COX2 inhibitor (coxib) class approved for use in horses (Equioxx) and for use in dogs (Previcox) Firocoxib was the first COX2 inhibitor approved by the US Food and Drug Administration for horses Firocoxib is not intended or approved for use in human medicine

Firocoxib manufactured by Merial was approved for veterinary use in the United States for dogs in July 2004 

Firocoxib is also available as a generic medication for horses and for dogs
