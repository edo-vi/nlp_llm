
A vingle is a portmanteau of video and single The neologism refers to music videos that can be beatmixing
The word was coined by a group of VJs in London in late 2003 at the This Is Clip Hop party during RESFest
An example vingle would be Hexstatics Timber (Coldcut and Hexstatic song)
Apple Computer has applied for a trademark on the term
