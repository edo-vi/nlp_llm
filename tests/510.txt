
CarnivacCov It is an inactivated vaccine for carnivores such as dogs cats foxes arctic foxes and mink to help prevent mutations in animals that can occur in crossspecies transmission

The vaccine is given in 2 doses 21 days apart In trials the tested animals sustained an immune response for at least six months The full course costs 500&nbsp;Russian ruble
Clinical trials on animals began in October 2020 The vaccine was registered in Russia on 31 March 2021 where production began on 30 April and administration began on 26 May
