Mavacoxib (trade name Trocoxil) is a veterinary drug used to treat pain and inflammation in dogs with degenerative joint
disease  It acts as a COX2 inhibitor

Mavacoxib along with other COX2 selective inhibitors celecoxib valdecoxib and parecoxib were discovered by a team at the GD Searle LLC
