The Dorn method is a form of manual HolismHolism in medicine
During a treatment the practitioner palpates the patients spine If any unbalanced areas are found possible underlying misalignments are palpated with gentle pressure using the  or hand against the spinous processes while the patient enacts guided movements such as swinging the leg or arms to distract the muscles inertia; this is similar to the principle of mechanics known as counter pressure In case of pain the patient is advised to stop the procedure in order to avoid any damage to the body
