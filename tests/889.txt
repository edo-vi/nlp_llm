Psychologs  is a monthly Mental Health print magazine publishing in English Language in Delhi India Editorial Board Members and mental health experts from various institutions/hospitals throw light on various issues of mental health in detail through this countrys only mental health magazine The magazine makes people aware by organizing programs on various psychological issues across the country The magazine provides a platform for mental health professionals to share their knowledge expertise and perspectives on various mental health topics with the general public through articles interviews and column and magazine considered as an authentic source about mental health in India

 Circulation and Readership

 Mental Health Wellbeing Awareness 
The magazine organises conferences meetings and events to promote mental health and wellbeing

 Mental Health Award 
Psychologs Magazines Mental Health Award is one the prestigious awards in the field of Mental Health and Psychology in India This award is given to the best people working for mental health awareness
