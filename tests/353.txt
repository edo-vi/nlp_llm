The Journal of EvidenceBased Integrative Medicine ((JEBIM)) published previously as the Journal of EvidenceBased Complementary and Alternative Medicine (JEBCAM) and also as Complementary Health Practice Review (CHPR) is a quarterly peerreviewed medical journal that covers hypothesisdriven and evidencebased research in the field of alternative medicine The editorinchief is Bruce Buehler (University of Nebraska Medical Center) The journal was established in 1995 and is published by SAGE Publications
