
Reflex locomotion usually referred to as the Vojta method is a technique for the treatment of physical and mental impairment in humans It was discovered by Václav Vojta

Based on studies of treatments for spastic children for muscular disorders in the 1950s a number of reflex points were identified which can be used to stimulate the human body to gait (human)Crawls
