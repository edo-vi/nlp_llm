Christian Veterinary Mission is a missionary sending organization founded by Leroy Dorminy in 1979 and based in Seattle Washington (US state)
As stated on their website "Christian Veterinary Mission seeks to help veterinarians serve others and live out their Christianity
There are many different aspects to the organizations ministry each supporting its purpose in a different way  Its primary activities involve sending and supporting veterinary missionaries both overseas and within the United States  These missionaries participate in various ministries depending on their location and personal gifts including: livestock development evangelism Ethnoveterinary medicine
Other Christian Veterinary Mission ministries include professional outreach and student outreach  The mission supports a Christian Veterinary fellowship student chapter at each of the 28 Veterinary school
Effective June 30 2021 CVM separated from CRISTA Ministries to become an independent nonprofit organization
