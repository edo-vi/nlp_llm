


Henry William SteeleBodger MRCVS (1896 &ndash; 1952) was a British veterinary surgeon

Educated at Cranleigh School he served with the Royal Engineers and Royal Horse Artillery He lost an eye in his war service  After the war he qualified as a vet at the Royal School of Veterinary Studies
In 1940 he  served as President of the British Veterinary Association
