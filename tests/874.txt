Qingfei Yihuo Wan () is a yellowishbrown honeyed pill used in Traditional Chinese medicine to "remove heat from the lungs relieve cough resolve phlegm and relax the bowels" It has a slight odor and tastes bitter It is used where there is "heat in the lung marked by cough yellowish sticky phlegm dryness of the mouth sore throat and constipation"
