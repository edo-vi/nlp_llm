
Fasting spittle&nbsp;– saliva produced first thing in the morning before breakfast&nbsp;– has been used to treat a wide variety of diseases for many hundreds of years Spittle cures are usually considered to be more effective if fasting spittle is used

An early recorded use of spittle as a cure comes from the Gospel of Mark
Writing at about the same time as Mark the Roman natural philosopher Pliny the Elder
