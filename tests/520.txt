

Bilious vomiting syndrome in dogs is vomiting in response to bileinduced inflammation of the stomach  It is also known as reflux gastritis syndrome and duodenalgastric reflux  Bile salts interfere with the gastric mucosal barrier allowing acid to irritate the stomach lining and cause gastritis
