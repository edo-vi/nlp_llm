  
Porcine epidemic diarrhea is a condition caused by the porcine epidemic diarrhea virus that leads to severe gastrointestinal disease in pigs

It is closely related to the agent responsible for Transmissible gastroenteritis coronavirus__TOC__
Signs and symptoms
In adult swine the disease is very mild and mortality is rare The primary signs are a watery diarrhea and mild systemic signs such as pyrexia Anorexia (symptom)Diagnosis
Diagnosis is via immunofluorescence or immunohistochemistry and ELISA can detect antigen or antibodies

Treatment and control
Treatment is symptomatic and aims to prevent dehydration in young pigs using products such as electrolyte and energy supplements Good biosecurity protocols such as adequate quarantine isolation of cases and disinfection help prevent entry or spread of the disease in the herd In Canada the Canadian Swine Health Board developed detailed protocols on how to adequately https://webarchiveorg/web/20150204193947/http://wwwthymoxcom/en/files/2014/03/LiveHogTransportVehicleWashDisinfectDryProtocolspdf  disinfect transportation vehicles for live hogs and ensure the https://webarchiveorg/web/20150204193859/http://wwwthymoxcom/en/files/2014/03/QualityControlofWashDisinfectDryProtocolsforLiveHogTransportVehiclespdf quality of the disinfection protocol
