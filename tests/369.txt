The National Literary Awards defines independent or "indie" literature as "books published outside mainstream publishing"http://nationalliteraryawardscom/americanbookchoiceaward/indexhtm Such books are rarely recognized and hard to pin down but some examples include "Damastor" by Dimitri Iatrou  "Returning Home" by Marcus Blake and "HopeJoy (and a Few Little Thoughts) for Pregnant Teens" by Rachel Brigoni
